One panel, every output

Run the whole show from one screen

ECP folds the three things every event needs - sound, screens, and the mic - into a single, no-scroll control panel built for live operation.

Audio

Music queue with local files, YouTube, and Spotify, plus a shared soundboard, crossfade, loop modes, breakpoints, and starred one-click sounds.

Visuals

A presenter view that drives a separate Display Window - images, video, PDFs, PowerPoint, and web embeds - with mirror preview, slide notes, Freeze, and Hide.

Intercom & Announce

Live or recorded mic routing with auto music ducking, soundboard cues, a shared clock/stopwatch/timer, and typed on-screen announcements.
